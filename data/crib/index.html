A Semesters Worth of Cribbage

Cribbage is a game of pairs, runs, sets of fifteens, mental strategy and luck, if you need a refresher check out the scoring guide.
While you are playing there is plenty of room to blunder, either from a miscount ⚠ or a misplay.
This dashboard serves as a place to dive back into previous games and analyze each and every hand, and see the outcomes averages and distributions of some real cribbage games.
