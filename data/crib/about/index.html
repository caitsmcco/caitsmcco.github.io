Scoring Guide

How to count your hand in cribbage

After each round of play, both players count the points in their hand (four cards + the cut card). Combinations can overlap — a single hand can score points in multiple categories simultaneously. The dealer also counts the crib as an extra hand.

Hand & play scoring
Fifteen Any combination of cards whose pip values sum to exactly 15. Face cards count as 10; Ace counts as 1. Score 2 for each distinct combination.
2 per combo
Pair Two cards of the same rank. Three of a kind scores 6 (three pairs). Four of a kind scores 12 (six pairs).
2 per pair
Run (Straight) Three or more cards in consecutive rank order. Suit does not matter. Score 1 point per card in the run.
1 per card
Flush Four cards in the hand of the same suit score 4. If the cut card also matches, score 5. In the crib, only a 5-card flush (including cut) counts.
4–5 pts
Nob (His Nobs) If your hand contains a Jack whose suit matches the cut card, score 1 point. Applies to both the hand and the crib.
1 pt
Pegging only
Thirty-one When the running total of played cards reaches exactly 31, score 2 points. The count then resets to zero.
2 pts
Go / Last Card If your opponent cannot play without exceeding 31, they say "Go" and you score 1 point. Playing the very last card also scores 1 (or 2 if it hits 31).
1 pt
His Heels If the dealer turns over a Jack as the cut card at the start of the round, the dealer immediately scores 2 points.
2 pts
Common combination hands
Tip: runs and pairs can overlap in the same hand, creating "double runs" and "triple runs" that score much more than they first appear. Always count systematically: fifteens first, then pairs, then runs, then flush, then nob.
Double run of 3 A pair plus two different 3-card runs built from those ranks. Two runs of 3 (6 pts) plus the pair (2 pts).
8 pts
Double run of 4 A pair plus two different 4-card runs. Two runs of 4 (8 pts) plus the pair (2 pts).
10 pts
Triple run of 3 Three of a kind plus three different 3-card runs. Three runs of 3 (9 pts) plus three of a kind (6 pts).
15 pts
Double-double run of 3 Two pairs plus four different 3-card runs. Four runs of 3 (12 pts) plus two pairs (4 pts).
16 pts
