In the following storyboard, the blue line and square on the snack box represent the latch on the inside of the door, locking it shut.

Situation 1: The Theif

1. A theif walks up looking for a treat, he grabs the remote from the holder
2. The theif presses three random buttons on the remote
3. The combonation is not accepted, the box remains locked and the theif gets jumpscared!

Situation 2: The Owner

1. The intended user wants a treat, he grabs the remote from the holder
2. They put in the combonation '5' '8' '1', the box unlatches the door
3. The door pops open and the user gets their treat
