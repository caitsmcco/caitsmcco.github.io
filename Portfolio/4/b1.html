Description

Tired of pesky snack stealers? Have a shared space where your treats are not safe? Jack is here to protect you! This arduino powered "snack in the box" will fend off theives with an arduino powered jump scare when the incorrect combonation is recieved. What is the right combination? None other than 581! The numeric identifier for this lovely class.

Implementation

Our Arduino used an IR sensor and remote, and two servos. The remote allowed us to give the user a multi-character input. The first servo operated as a latch to hold the door in a locked posiiton. If the arduino recieved the correct input sequence this servo would rotate, allowing the door to pop open and reward the user with a snack. After an interval of time, the servo would attempt to relatch the door. Idealy, I think it would have been best to replace this timed mechanism with a light sensor, and I will touch on this more later. The second servo would trigger when the arduino recieved an incorrect input sequence, and triggered the abrupt launch of a scary joker to tell them off before slowly sculking back into it's hiding spot.
