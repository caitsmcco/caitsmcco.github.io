I had a very advantageous plan for this project, and oh boy was it necessary to reduce the scope. In my initial plan, I wanted the device to keep track of
- multiple household members and their location in the home
- groceries, and all current items in the fridge for meal recommendations
- how many dirty dishes there were
- fully create an animate TWO friendly companions
- use multiple devices that would communicate
The feedback I received from peers went along the lines of "you're insane, what do you mean you wanna do all that?!?!?" So I headed back to the drawing board. Initially, I really wanted to focus on the dishes. A device that would know how many dishes you had, reassure you that they really wouldn't take that long, and put on a nice timer and some music. This idea was still heavily centered around the idea of a friendly companion. This still would have required A LOT of work, between creating a full character and somehow measuring dirty, clean and dry dishes and the users activity there simply was not enough time or resources to make it a reality. (but I was still hooked on little companions, I love little guys, it was my downfall)
As time passed and I drove this thought train through my daily routine, I had an idea of a Tamagotchi rip off, where taking care of your kitchen would take care of your friend. But, at this point I decided to scrap the animated companion idea completely... Rest in piece my little time wasters...
So then what? What was I really trying to do?? Without the warm, comforting light of a friend, I turned to inspire the user through music - after all there is nothing better than waking up to calm music. I am a massive fan of the Zelda soundtrack (and am praying that the Nintendo gods do not smite me for using it in this small, unpublished, project). I finalized my main, general design for the main screen, displayed in the center of the below image.
I decided that I wanted to focus on meal encouragement, as conversations with friends revealed that I wasn't the only person that wouldn't hold myself accountable for skipping meals, and it was time for me to create a solution...
